OptiCrop

About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is a Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine designed to recommend the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions.

Objectives

  • Provide intelligent crop recommendations.
  • Optimize water and fertilizer usage.
  • Reduce crop failure.

Technology Stack

Python (Flask), SQLite, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5.

Benefits

Improved yield, cost efficiency, and sustainable farming practices.
